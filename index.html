The Wolverine Spirit

The Wolverine Math Tournament (WMT) is more than just a test; it's a celebration of curiosity. Organized by the Westview Math Club, we invite 3rd–8th graders to experience a day of collaborative problem-solving and high-stakes buzzer rounds.

Grades 3–5 Elementary
Grades 6–8 Middle School

Ⅰ

Individual Round

A 45-minute sprint focusing on speed and accuracy. No calculators, just pure logic.

Ⅱ

Team Round

Work with 4-5 teammates to solve complex puzzles that require collective brainstorming.

Ⅲ

Buzzer Round

The top performers go head-to-head in a live, fast-paced elimination bracket.
